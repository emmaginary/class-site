The Layered Cut

Divide the hair on top into a horizontal one inch section on either side of the head to begin cutting the layers. Lift your freshly cut guideline at a 90 degree angle above the head. Comb your one inch section upwards, holding both your guide and your section with your fingers.

(scroll to see demo)
Trim your one inch section to your guideline. Work your way through the section until complete.
